About Computer Paradise

Your trusted laptop partner in Mysore since 2021

Our Story

Computer Paradise was founded in 2021 with a simple mission — to make quality laptops accessible and affordable for everyone in Mysore. Located at 134, KT Street, Lashkar Mohalla, we've grown from a small setup to one of the most trusted laptop resellers in the city.

Every laptop we sell goes through a rigorous 15-point quality check — testing the battery, display, keyboard, ports, performance, and more. We grade each device transparently (A/B/C) so you know exactly what you're getting, with no hidden surprises.

We believe in fair prices, honest advice, and great service. Whether you're buying or selling, we treat you like family.
